The Final Event

The final awards event will be hosted at London Connected Learning Centre on 16th June 2016. At this event pupils will get a chance to play each other’s games. Teams will also have to deliver their presentations to the panel of judges.

Schools can bring a maximum of 6 pupils to the final event and submit a maximum of two games.

Awards

At the final event judges will review all the games and award prizes in the following categories.

  • Best Game: overall winner
  • Best Story
  • Best Visual Design (judged on game design and poster)
  • Best Programming
  • Best Presentation
  • All pupil votes on the day will counted and a People’s Choice winner will be announced

Submit your games

All games must be submitted by email, to jhalloran@londonclc.org.uk by 3.30pm on Friday 10th June 2016.
